About Me

I received a BA in history from the College of William and Mary in September of 2017, then completed a professional studies program at George Washington University. I am a skilled writer; conscientious, organized, and goal-oriented worker. I am well and widely-read and up-to-date on the industry and broader culture. I am passionate about running, cycling, and reading, especially tech news, favorite blogs, short stories, and horror.
