About Me

Full stack web developer with a focus on databases and React.js. After receiving a degree in history, I decided to pursue my life-long passion for programming professionally. I received a BA in history from the College of William and Mary in September of 2017, then completed a professional studies program at George Washington University's School of Professional Studies. I am a skilled technical writer; conscientious, organized, and goal-oriented worker. I am well and widely-read and up-to-date on the industry and broader culture. In my spare time, I tutor eighth graders and participate as an adult leader in BSA Troop 624. Beyond tech, I am passionate about teaching, running, cycling, reading, and my cats and dog.
